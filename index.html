Kumon Fishermall is a locally operated center under the globally recognized Kumon franchise, located inside Fisher Mall along Quezon Avenue, Quezon City. We have been proudly serving families in the area and helping children unlock their full academic potential.

We believe in creating an encouraging and disciplined environment where students not only excel academically but also develop the self-learning habits that will serve them for life.

The Kumon Method

Proven. Global.
Self-paced.

The Kumon Method is a globally recognized learning system that focuses on individualized instruction and self-learning. Each student begins at a level where they can succeed independently, building confidence before gradually progressing.

Rather than teaching for short-term exam performance, Kumon develops long-term academic ability by strengthening concentration, time management, and perseverance — essential skills for success in school and beyond.

Founded in Japan in 1958 by Toru Kumon, the method was originally created by a father who wanted to help his own son excel in mathematics. Today, Kumon operates in over 50 countries and has helped more than 4 million students reach their full potential.

📐
Individualized Instruction

Every student has a unique starting point and progresses at their own pace — never rushed, never held back.

🔁
Repetition & Mastery

Concepts are repeated and refined until full mastery is achieved, ensuring no gaps in understanding before advancing.

🧠
Self-Learning

Worksheets are designed so students learn by doing — developing the ability to think and solve independently.

"The goal of Kumon is not just to advance students academically, but to nurture their potential and cultivate a love of learning that lasts a lifetime."

— Toru Kumon, Founder
Kumon Connect

Same trusted method.
Now on tablet.

Kumon Connect is Kumon's official digital learning platform — giving instructors real-time insights into student performance and allowing for faster, more precise feedback without ever compromising the integrity of the Kumon Method.

💻
Digital Learning (Kumon Connect)
  • Worksheets completed on tablet with stylus
  • Instant submission and faster instructor feedback
  • Real-time progress tracking & performance monitoring
  • No need to carry physical worksheets
  • Secure cloud-based storage of all completed work
  • Convenient for both in-center and home study
  • Eco-friendly — paperless learning environment
📄
Traditional Paper Worksheets
  • Familiar and structured learning format
  • Strong handwriting & fine motor skill development
  • Physical tracking of completed worksheet bundles
  • Minimal screen exposure — ideal for young children
  • Proven method used globally for over 60 years
  • Ideal for students who prefer tactile learning
  • No device or internet connection required
